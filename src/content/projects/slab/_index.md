Slab is the writing tool I wanted but couldn't find: a fast, native, markdown-first editor that opens instantly and gets out of the way. No plugin system, no AI sidebar, no kitchen-sink "workspace" — just a CodeMirror buffer in a Tauri shell with the spacing and shortcuts dialed in.

Every decision follows a small set of explicit principles documented in the repo. The hard part of a project like this isn't building features; it's saying no to them.
