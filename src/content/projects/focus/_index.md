status: live
started: 2026-03
---

GNOME panel indicators that try to live next to the centered clock usually break or get bumped. This one is "clock-safe" — it stays put through workspace switches and extension reloads, and drives a pomodoro session that flips the blocker script on for the focus interval and off for the break.
