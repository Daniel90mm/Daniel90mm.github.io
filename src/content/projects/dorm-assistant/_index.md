title: Dorm Assistant
summary: Toolkit built around a spare OnePlus Nord 2 5G running stock Android 13 — wireless-first dorm utilities, given the phone's unreliable charging port.
status: live
started: 2026-02
---

The constraint is the fun part: bootloader locked, charging port flaky, no root. So everything has to work over the wireless surface — Tasker, Termux, ADB-over-Wi-Fi. The dorm-assistant repo is the collection of scripts and shortcuts that turn that phone into a useful always-on helper without ever plugging it in.
